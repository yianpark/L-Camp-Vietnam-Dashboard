ESG 임팩트 및 롯데 협업을 통한 임팩트 창출
KO-MAPPER's KO-Detect Pro directly addresses three of the most pressing ESG dimensions in Vietnam's rapidly urbanizing built environment: Environmental: Vietnam is building at an unprecedented pace — yet the construction and infrastructure sector remains one of the country's largest sources of material waste, carbon emissions, and environmental degradation. Undetected structural deterioration in Vietnam's tropical climate (where heat, humidity, and monsoon conditions accelerate concrete corrosion and rebar exposure at rates far exceeding temperate markets) leads to premature structural failure, emergency demolition, and carbon-intensive reconstruction cycles. KO-Detect Pro's AI-driven early-stage defect detection and time-series predictive maintenance capabilities directly counter this trajectory — extending facility lifecycles, dramatically reducing unnecessary material consumption, and supporting Vietnam's commitment to its 2050 net-zero carbon neutrality target as pledged at COP26. A PoC with LOTTE E&C Vietnam would generate concrete, quantifiable data on lifecycle extension and carbon footprint reduction per building — data that LOTTE can leverage directly in its ESG disclosures and green financing applications, particularly as Vietnam's green bond market and ESG-linked real estate investment frameworks continue to mature. Social: Vietnam's construction boom has come with a deeply troubling human cost. The country consistently ranks among Southeast Asia's highest for construction-site fatalities and occupational injuries, while high-profile structural failures — collapsing bridges, crumbling overpasses, and deteriorating apartment buildings in aging urban districts — continue to claim lives and displace communities. These failures disproportionately affect Vietnam's most vulnerable urban populations, who rely on aging public infrastructure and affordable housing that receives the least maintenance investment. KO-MAPPER's solution directly confronts this crisis by replacing dangerous, subjective manual inspections — conducted by workers on ropes, scaffolding, and aerial lifts in Vietnam's extreme heat and monsoon conditions — with safe, drone-based AI diagnostics that reduce occupational injury risk by up to 96% (based on RPN index). A LOTTE collaboration would amplify this social impact at scale: protecting construction workers across LOTTE's Vietnam project portfolio, delivering objectively safer living environments to thousands of LOTTE residential customers in Vietnam, and contributing to the Vietnamese government's stated national priority of reducing construction-related fatalities — positioning LOTTE as a genuine corporate citizen and safety leader in the Vietnamese market, not merely a foreign developer extracting value. Governance: Vietnam's regulatory environment for construction safety and facility management is undergoing rapid formalization, driven by the government's push to attract foreign direct investment, meet international financing standards (World Bank, ADB, IFC), and align with ASEAN-wide infrastructure governance frameworks. International investors and development finance institutions increasingly require rigorous, auditable safety documentation as a precondition for project financing — a requirement that Vietnam's largely manual, paper-based inspection system currently cannot reliably fulfill. KO-Detect Pro's automated, tamper-resistant digital inspection reports and time-series audit trails provide LOTTE Vietnam with a governance framework that simultaneously satisfies Vietnamese regulatory requirements and meets the documentation standards demanded by international capital markets. By integrating KO-MAPPER's platform across its Vietnam facility portfolio, LOTTE would establish the most transparent, digitally governed real estate and construction operation in Vietnam — a decisive competitive advantage in attracting ESG-conscious international investors, securing green financing at preferential rates, and building the institutional trust with Vietnamese authorities that underpins long-term market leadership. A PoC collaboration between KO-MAPPER and LOTTE in Vietnam would not merely validate technology in a new market — it would create a globally replicable model for how multinational conglomerates can operationalize ESG commitments in high-growth, high-risk emerging market construction environments, generating both measurable impact metrics and a compelling ESG narrative that strengthens LOTTE's sustainability reporting, investor relations, and social license to operate across Southeast Asia.

Vision AI 모델의 성능은 학습 데이터의 다양성(구조물 재질·환경·손상 유형)에 크게 의존하므로, 베트남·파나마 등 신규 시장 진출 시 현지 인프라 특성(예: 고온다습 환경의 콘크리트 열화, 해안 부식)을 반영한 재학습 및 라벨링 작업이 필수적 [미확인: 시장별 데이터 확보 계획 부재]. 현지 건설사·발주처로부터 라벨링된 결함 샘플을 확보하는 데 수개월~1년 이상 소요될 수 있으며, 이는 PoC 일정 지연 및 초기 정확도 하락으로 이어질 가능성 높음 [미확인]. 글로벌 일반화 모델 개발 로드맵 및 전이 학습(transfer learning) 전략 제시 필요.
