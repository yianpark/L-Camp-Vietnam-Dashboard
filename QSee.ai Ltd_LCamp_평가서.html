ESG 임팩트 및 롯데 협업을 통한 임팩트 창출
QSee.ai creates ESG impact by reducing waste, rework, returns, and manual quality inconsistency in apparel manufacturing. By replacing manual inspection with real-time AI quality control, we help manufacturers catch defects earlier, reduce off-spec production, and avoid unnecessary material, labor, and energy loss. This supports the environmental side through lower waste and fewer returned or reworked garments, the social side through more standardized and less fatigue-dependent QC processes, and the governance side through transparent, structured quality data shared between brands and factories. A PoC with LOTTE could demonstrate this impact in a real supply-chain environment by validating how AI-based QC improves consistency, reduces defect leakage, and creates measurable quality data across manufacturing operations. If successful, the PoC could become the basis for broader rollout across LOTTE’s apparel and retail ecosystem, creating both operational and ESG value at scale.
