ESG 임팩트 및 롯데 협업을 통한 임팩트 창출
AlgoXpert / NEXUS addresses ESG impact primarily through responsible AI usage, financial transparency, and risk-awareness infrastructure. As AI-generated financial logic becomes increasingly accessible, there is growing risk around opaque decision-making, overfitting, hidden fragility, and uncontrolled automation in financial systems. NEXUS focuses on verification, explainability, and failure detection before capital or operational decisions are exposed to unnecessary risk. From an ESG perspective, our solution contributes to: - More transparent and explainable AI-assisted financial processes - Reduction of hidden systemic and operational risk - Responsible deployment of AI in financial environments - Improved governance through evidence-based verification workflows - Better long-term financial resilience and trust A collaboration with LOTTE could help validate how verification infrastructure can support safer and more accountable AI adoption within enterprise and financial environments, while also contributing to stronger governance and operational transparency.
