ESG 임팩트 및 롯데 협업을 통한 임팩트 창출
1. Environmental Impact We aim to significantly reduce CO₂ emissions by accelerating the adoption of electric vehicles (EVs) in Vietnam. Currently, approximately 60% of total CO₂ emissions in Vietnam come from electricity & heat generation and the transportation sector, which poses a major challenge to achieving the country’s Net Zero 2050 commitment. Beyond emissions, Vietnam is also facing a macro-level energy challenge - electricity shortages and rising energy costs. According to the World Bank, around 3.5 million people in Vietnam are affected by electricity shortages annually, resulting in approximately $32 million in additional electricity costs each year. This has a direct negative impact on both economic activity and quality of life. Volterra addresses these challenges by optimizing energy consumption and electricity costs for EV charging operators through our smart energy management and optimization platform. By improving efficiency at the infrastructure level, we create a systematic positive impact across the entire ecosystem, from operators to EV users, and ultimately to the broader community. 2. Social Impact Our solution contributes to improving energy accessibility and affordability, while enabling a smoother transition toward sustainable mobility. By reducing the strain on the national power grid and lowering operational costs, we help make EV adoption more practical and accessible for a wider population. 3. Role of PoC Collaboration with LOTTE A Proof of Concept (PoC) with LOTTE affiliates (such as malls, hotels, and commercial properties) would allow us to: - Deploy and validate our energy optimization platform at scale within high-traffic, energy-intensive environments - Reduce energy consumption, peak load pressure, and electricity costs across LOTTE’s properties - Integrate EV charging infrastructure with smart energy management systems, creating a model for sustainable commercial real estate - Demonstrate measurable CO₂ reduction and operational efficiency improvements, which can be scaled across LOTTE’s ecosystem and beyond Through this collaboration, LOTTE can become a leading example of sustainable infrastructure transformation in Vietnam, while Volterra delivers measurable ESG impact aligned with both corporate sustainability goals and Vietnam's Net Zero 2050 Mission.
