ESG 임팩트 및 롯데 협업을 통한 임팩트 창출
LandaX addresses all three pillars of ESG through its digital real estate ecosystem — marketplace, Smart CRM, and AI assistant — and a PoC with LOTTE accelerates measurable impact across each dimension. Environmental: Vietnam's traditional real estate market relies heavily on physical site visits, paper-based documentation, and repeated in-person meetings to close a single transaction. LandaX digitizes the entire transaction lifecycle — virtual property tours, e-contracts, digital due diligence — helping reduce travel, paper usage, and manual coordination associated with traditional real estate transactions. More importantly, LandaX's AI-powered matching engine optimizes occupancy rates for commercial assets, helping improve occupancy utilization and reduce inefficiencies associated with underutilized commercial assets. A PoC with LOTTE's retail and hospitality portfolio could help establish operational benchmarks related to digital workflows, occupancy utilization, and sustainability reporting. Social: Commercial real estate investment in Vietnam has historically been accessible only to large institutional players or well-connected individuals. LandaX democratizes access by providing a transparent marketplace and an AI advisor available 24/7 — removing the information asymmetry that disadvantages retail investors and SMEs. LOTTE's customer base of millions of middle-class Vietnamese consumers represents an untapped audience for quality real estate investment opportunities. Through this PoC, LOTTE can create genuine social value by giving its loyal customers access to real estate assets they manage — improving access to transparent investment information for SMEs and middle-income consumers. Governance: Vietnam's real estate market faces persistent challenges around pricing transparency, legal documentation integrity, and transaction history verification. LandaX standardizes property data, digitizes legal records, and creates a full audit trail for every transaction — reducing fraud risk and legal disputes. LandaX's Smart CRM also enables LOTTE to manage its entire asset portfolio with ESG-aligned data reporting, meeting the growing disclosure requirements from Vietnamese regulators and LOTTE Group's own Korean corporate governance standards. A PoC collaboration provides LOTTE with a tested, scalable framework for ESG-compliant asset management across its Vietnam operations.
