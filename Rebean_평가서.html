ESG 임팩트 및 롯데 협업을 통한 임팩트 창출
Social OTA platforms charge hotels 15–25% commission per booking. For independent and family-owned hotels that form the backbone of Vietnam's tourism economy, this directly compresses margins and limits reinvestment into staff and local communities. Rebean drives more direct bookings by optimising hotel visibility in AI search, keeping commission revenue with the hotel. A PoC with Lotte's Vietnam properties would make this impact quantifiable at enterprise scale. Governance Hotels currently have no visibility into what AI tells millions of travellers about them. ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity actively recommend hotels based on information the hotel cannot see, correct, or control. Rebean provides the daily monitoring and audit layer that closes this gap. A collaboration with Lotte, a group with established ESG reporting standards, would help set an early industry norm for responsible AI brand governance in Southeast Asian hospitality.
